So, you want to talk to space?

You're in luck! You have a SPACE WALKIE TALKIE. But there's a problem. It only has few milliseconds of battery remaining: just barely enough to send one message out, and receive one signal in return.

Here's how it works. Click "send" to send your message, then "receive" when you think your signal will arrive back at Earth. If you're within +/- 25 milliseconds of the signal arriving back on Earth, you win! Otherwise... better luck next time.

Tip #1: your signal will move at the speed of light!

Tip #2: things are far away.
